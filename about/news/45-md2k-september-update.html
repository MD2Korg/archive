News

MD2K September Update

We are almost halfway through Year 2, and it’s very clear that the entire MD2K team has been busy! The first studies have been launched, and the center is approaching its first software release in December. Papers have been accepted and presented at multiple conferences as MD2K projects move forward. As we prepare for the MD2K Annual Meeting (Sept. 14-16), here is a rundown on the team’s accomplishments for the period between April 2015 and September 2015:

We have had two late-breaking news events this week:

Best Paper Award at MobiCom 2015

CAreDroid: Adaptation Framework for Android ContextAware Applications, co-authored by Salma Elmalaki, Lucas Wanner and Mani Srivastava of UCLA, won the Best Paper and Best Community Paper awards at MobiCom 2015, currently underway (Sept. 7-11) in Paris, France.

Thomaz accepts position at U. of Texas

Edison Thomaz, Ph.D. candidate at Georgia Tech, has accepted the position of Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin.

Other Recognition/Honors

Polo Chau, Georgia Tech

  • August 2015: Received Google Faculty Research Award
  • 2015: Received Outstanding Junior Faculty Research Award. College of Computing, Georgia Tech.
  • 2015-16: Received the James Edenfield Faculty Fellowship, with Munmun De Choudhury (IC)

Santosh Kumar, University of Memphis

  • Summer 2015: Named to the Lillian & Morrie Moss Chair of Excellence, the first Chair of Excellence in Computer Science at the University of Memphis

Ida Sim, University of California-San Francisco, Open mHealth

  • Appointed Chair of the Advisory Panel for the World Health Organization’s International Clinical Trials Registry Platform
  • Named Co-Chair of the IT Workgroup of the MRCT Data Sharing and Transparency Project

Recent Personnel Changes

David E. Conroy has taken a position at Pennsylvania State University as Professor of Kinesiology; he was previously at Northwestern University.

Shahin Samiei has joined the MD2K staff as Research Data & Study Coordinator

Kevin O. Moran, MPH, Research Study Coordinator Senior in the Department of Preventive Medicine at Northwestern University, has joined the team.

Moushumi Sharmin, PhD., has taken a position as Assistant Professor in the College of Science and  Engineering at Western Washington University in Bellingham. She previously was at the University of Memphis.

In the Media

Emre Ertin, Santosh Kumar, Ida Sim

  • Featured in Wearing Your Health on Your Sleeve, the lead article in the June 2015 issue of Biomedical Computational Review, published by the Stanford Mobilize Center. Read it here: http://www.biomedicalcomputationreview.org/content/wearing-your-health-your-sleeve

Santosh Kumar

  • Interviewed Aug. 12 by Karen Jagoda for Empowered Patient Radio, a program affiliated with Scripps Translational Science Institute.

Ida Sim
